FAQ

Common questions about AI voice agents and AEO

What does Points Beyond AI do?

Points Beyond AI builds AI voice agents that answer your business phone 24/7 and delivers Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) strategies that position your business to be cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview. What is an AI Voice Agent and how does it work for my business?

An AI Voice Agent answers your business phone 24/7 with natural, human-sounding conversation — not a phone tree, not voicemail. It books appointments directly into your calendar, answers FAQs about your services, qualifies leads, and delivers a full transcript and summary of every call to your email or CRM. Unlike a human receptionist, it never misses a call and costs a fraction of the staffing cost.

How much does an AI Voice Agent cost?

Our AI Voice Agent starts at $399/month, which includes configuration, calendar integration, CRM connection, and ongoing support. We offer a free 7-day trial so you can see the results before you commit — no credit card required. Most clients recover the full monthly cost within the first week through leads that would have otherwise gone to voicemail.

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and why does it matter?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your content so that AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview cite your business when users ask relevant questions. As AI-powered search replaces traditional blue-link results, AEO is how businesses stay visible to the growing share of customers who ask AI directly instead of searching Google. If you're not optimized for AI answers, you're invisible to that audience.

What is the difference between AEO and traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for ranking in Google's blue-link results — users still have to click through to your website. AEO optimizes for being the answer AI systems generate directly, without the user visiting any website. Both matter: SEO drives traffic from people who search and click; AEO captures the growing share of users who ask AI assistants and accept the generated answer. We deliver both.

How quickly can you deploy an AI Voice Agent?

We deploy most AI Voice Agents within 7 days of the kickoff call. That includes configuration, integration with your calendar and CRM, training on your specific FAQs and services, and a live test run before handoff. You can be capturing leads you currently miss within one week of our first conversation.

What types of businesses benefit most from an AI Voice Agent?

Any business that receives inbound calls and loses revenue when those calls go unanswered. Home services (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, cleaning), healthcare practices, law firms, real estate agencies, salons, contractors, and professional services firms all see strong ROI. If a missed call means a missed customer, an AI Voice Agent pays for itself quickly.
