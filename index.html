FAQ

Common questions about AI automation for small business

What does Points Beyond AI actually do?

Points Beyond AI builds custom AI applications, AI voice agents, and workflow automation systems for small and medium businesses. We identify where your business is losing time and money to manual processes, then design and deploy AI systems that eliminate those bottlenecks. Most clients see measurable ROI within 60–90 days.

How much does AI automation cost for a small business?

Our AI Voice Receptionist starts at $599/month. Custom AI apps and automation workflows are priced per project, typically $2,500–$15,000 depending on complexity. Most clients recoup their investment within 60–90 days through time saved and leads recovered. The strategy call is free — we'll give you a clear estimate before you commit to anything.

How long does it take to build and deploy a custom AI app?

Most custom AI apps and automation workflows are delivered in 2–4 weeks from the kickoff call. Simple automations can go live in under a week. Complex multi-system integrations may take 4–8 weeks. We move fast because every week of delay has a real cost to your business.

What is an AI Voice Agent and how does it work for my business?

An AI Voice Agent answers your business phone 24/7 with natural, human-sounding conversation — no rigid phone tree, no voicemail. It books appointments directly into your calendar, answers FAQs, qualifies leads, and routes urgent calls to you. You receive a full transcript and summary of every call to your email or CRM.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage the AI systems you build?

No. We handle all technical implementation, configuration, and integration. We train your team on how to use the systems we build and provide ongoing support. If something needs adjusting after launch, you contact us — not a software vendor. We stay accountable for making it work.

What types of businesses benefit most from AI automation?

Service businesses, professional services firms, healthcare practices, real estate agencies, contractors, and any SMB generating $300K+ in annual revenue with 2–50 employees. If you have repetitive processes or staff spending hours on tasks that don't require human judgment — AI automation will deliver measurable ROI.

How does AI automation actually increase revenue?

Three ways: (1) Leads that went cold while you were busy are now responded to within minutes — faster response means higher close rates. (2) Your AI Voice Agent converts calls that would have gone to voicemail into booked appointments. (3) Your team spends less time on admin and more time on revenue-generating activities. Most clients see ROI within 60 days.

Is my business data secure with AI systems from Points Beyond AI?

Yes. We build on enterprise-grade platforms with bank-level encryption and zero-retention policies for sensitive data where required. We hold an active TS/SCI clearance and apply federal-grade security standards to every engagement. We never use your business data to train AI models.
